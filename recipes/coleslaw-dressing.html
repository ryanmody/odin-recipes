Coleslaw Dressing

Description

This creamy, mayo-based dressing uses ingredients you likely have on hand: sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, pepper, and of course, mayonnaise.

The resulting dressing is just the right balance of sweet, tangy, creamy, and peppery!

Ingredients

    ½ cup mayonnaise
    2 tablespoons white sugar
    1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
    1 tablespoon vinegar
    ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
    ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

    Gather all ingredients
    Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
    Store in an airtight container.
